Diagonal Smile

A diagonal smile is a small, subtle smile where the corners of the mouth lift slightly but unevenly, often creating a playful or cheeky expression. It’s less obvious than a full smile and can suggest a mix of emotions like amusement, slyness, or mild happiness.

Phillip Massey

8 min

Sad smile

A sad smile is when someone smiles but their eyes or mouth show a bit of sadness or tiredness. It’s like they’re trying to be happy but you can still tell something’s bothering them underneath. It’s a mix of feeling down but wanting to stay positive.

Phillip Massey

8 min

Natural Smile

A natural smile is a genuine, spontaneous smile that comes from real happiness or positive feelings. It usually involves the whole face, including the eyes, which often crinkle at the corners, showing true emotion. It looks relaxed and effortless, unlike forced or polite smiles.

Phillip Massey

8 min

Happy Smile

A happy smile is when someone’s face lights up with joy! It usually shows with big, upturned lips and bright eyes that might even sparkle. It’s the kind of smile that makes you feel warmth and good vibes right away.

Phillip Massey

8 min
